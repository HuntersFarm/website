# Hunters Farm

<p>Hunters Farm consists of 23 acres in the High Weald of East Sussex.&nbsp; We have a small flock of pedigree Shetland sheep.</p>

<p>These slow-growing, 100% grass-fed lambs are slaughtered at between 1 and 2 years old for the tastiest, tenderest meat.</p>

<p>We sell a whole lamb for £113 or half lamb for £62.&nbsp; All the meat is professionally butchered and vacuum-packed.&nbsp; Available fresh by appointment or frozen, subject to availability, all year round.</p>
